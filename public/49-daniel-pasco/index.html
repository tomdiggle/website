Daniel Pasco (@dlpasco) is the CEO at Black Pixel, a secret band of iOS and Mac Development ninja assassins. In this episode, Saul chats with Daniel about being a Ninja Assassin, a Rocket Scientist, a Musician and occasionally, a software Developer. We also chat about Black Pixel’s recent software acquisitions, releases and other random stuff that came up.
