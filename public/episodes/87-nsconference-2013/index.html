This week, we attended NSConference 2013 in Leicester, UK. In this episode, special guest Paul Darcey and host Saul Mora discuss the atmosphere, vibe and experience of NSConference. Saul also chats with Peter Steinberger on his experience in selling a PDK library to iOS developers. We then head up to Edinburgh, Scotland and talk with husband and wife team, Jamie Montgomerie and Emily Thomforde about their upcoming game “Coolson’s Artisanal Chocolate Alphabet”.
