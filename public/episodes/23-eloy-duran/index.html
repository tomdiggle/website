Eloy Duran recently gave a talk about MacRuby for SecondConf 2011 in Chicago. Eloy is a MacRuby core team member and consultant at Fingertips in The Netherlands. Saul and Eloy discussed the MacRuby project, as well as Eloy’s new cocoa source dependency manager, CocoaPods.
