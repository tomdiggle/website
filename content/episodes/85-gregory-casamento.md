[Greg Casamento](http://heronsperch.blogspot.com/) ([@bheron](http://twitter.com/bheron)) is the lead maintainer of the GNUStep project, which started as an open source implementation of many of the original NEXT frameworks, and has grown to include many of the Cocoa frameworks you may be using in your projects. Included in this discussion is some of the history of NeXT, OpenStep and, sadly enough, Java.
