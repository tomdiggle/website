This week, we attended [NSConference](http://nsconference.com) 2013 in Leicester, UK. In this episode, special guest [Paul Darcey](http://twitter.com/pauldarcey) and host Saul Mora discuss the atmosphere, vibe and experience of NSConference. Saul also chats with [Peter Steinberger](http://twitter.com/steipete) on his experience in selling a PDK library to iOS developers. We then head up to Edinburgh, Scotland and talk with husband and wife team, [Jamie Montgomerie](http://twitter.com/th_in_gs) and [Emily Thomforde](http://twitter.com/thegrene) about their upcoming game ["Coolson's Artisanal Chocolate Alphabet"](http://coolsons.com).
